About Welham Boys' School

Welham Boys' School fosters academic discipline, leadership, and intellectual rigor. The institution encourages exploration beyond conventional curricula, creating space for scientific curiosity and independent thought.

The Astronomy Club operates within this environment of structured excellence, transforming theoretical science into observational experience.

About The Club

The Welham Astronomy Club is a student-led initiative focused on observational astronomy, astrophotography, and structured research.

Through systematic observation nights and guided inquiry, members engage with celestial mechanics and astronomical phenomena in a disciplined framework.
